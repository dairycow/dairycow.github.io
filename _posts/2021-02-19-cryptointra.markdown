Last nights trading session was very messy. Got lucky with a short. 

Today/tonight trading was mostly poor, except for BNB long off VWAP/250. 

With that out of the way, I am going to focus on the positives. My goal is to trade less aggressively for my account size.

### BNB
Tried shorting this throughout the Asia session. For these parabolic shorts I really need to wait for it to blowoff and properly decay. 

Came back to BNB this evening and saw that it had gone completely parabolic. 

Backside faded a little through 270. This was a very slow trade that ultimately ended up working but my size was too big.

It got down to about 254 where it refused to break. Held VWAP and then there was a battle on the tape. After viewing the strength of this and that it was absorbing whatever was being thrown at it, I flipped long with a tight stop. This was a very high conviction spot for me and I added some good size for my account and made a nice move.

### QTUM
Good spot to be short around round number and when the daily chart is on the backside. With the volatility of crypto, I should wait and get in through the round number rather than getting in beforehand. The extra few cents add a lot to your edge.
