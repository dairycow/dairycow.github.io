Summary of Material Information from ASX Announcement

Metallurgical Testwork Results (Key Takeaways)

  • Oxide & Transition Material:
  • Excellent gold recoveries (>95%) using standard Gravity Gold / Carbon in Leach (GG/CIL) processing.
  • Fresh Material:
  • Poor recoveries (below 70%) with GG/CIL alone due to refractory gold in arsenopyrite.
  • Flotation + GG/CIL significantly improved recovery to 91.2% (5.8% gravity, 60.4% flotation, 25.0% leach).
  • Requires additional processing (e.g., ultra-fine grinding, bacterial oxidation, roasting) but unlocks value.

Exploration & Next Steps

  • Upcoming Drilling:
  • 2025 field season drilling at Hyperion, including two deep diamond holes funded by Northern Territory Government.
  • Targets: Down-dip/down-plunge extensions of Tethys lode (follow-up on high-grade intercept: 10m @ 15.9g/t Au).
  • Further Testwork Planned:
  • Heap leach potential for Oxide/Transition material.
  • Fine grinding studies for Fresh material flotation concentrate.

Mineral Resource Context

  • Hyperion Deposit:
  • Total Resource (April 2025): 9.66Mt @ 1.4g/t Au (435koz).
  • Cut-off: 0.5g/t Au (Oxide/Transition), 0.6g/t Au (Fresh).

Regulatory Progress

  • Mining Lease Application: Submitted December 2024 (estimated 2-year approval timeline).

Conclusion

Testwork confirms robust recoveries for Oxide/Transition material, while Fresh material requires more complex processing. Upcoming drilling and further metallurgical studies aim to optimize resource economics. No immediate capital-raising or trading halt indicated.
